The Famous Home Page

Yes, this is the first page, or as you might call the “home” page. Why does the first page need to be first? Who knows, but it is the first, even thou sometimes it’s not the first page that a user usually sees on your website.

So, why the first page is so important? Well, you might say because it’s the first duh !!!. We guess that as with many things in life the first impression is really important. So, that’s why the first page needs to be the first and it needs to be neat.

Then, what happens when you start browsing other pages and find out they are better, and offer tons of relevant information that the first page doesn’t, then, shouldn’t the first page be some kind of simple display that allows you to go further in your browsing history on the site.
